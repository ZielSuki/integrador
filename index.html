About hardcore music

Hardcore punk is a music genre and subculture that originated in the 1970s. It's faster, harder, and more aggressive than early punk. It was inspired by Washington D.C. and New York punk and early proto-punk. Hardcore punk generally disavows commercialism, the music industry and often addresses social and political topics with "confrontational, politically-charged lyrics."

About us

We are a group of hardcore punks who wants to share information about bands of the past and bands of today, and so we made a webpage that will function as a type of archive of hardcore punk bands, people, fanzines, etc. The Fanzine and People section is still being constructed and at the same time we are updating our band archive. We hope you'll find some new good music!
